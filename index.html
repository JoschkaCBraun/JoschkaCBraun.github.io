I am a Machine Learning MSc student at the University of Tübingen, working in the Health-NLP group Tübingen.

Interests: I am interested in using Deep Learnin in NLP, to create AI systems that are effective, explainable, and socially beneficial. As AI capabilities are steadily increasing, I am interested in how to address and mitigate the societal risks associated with AI technologies both with technical and governance approaches.

Bio: I received my BSc degree in Computer Science from the Unviersity of Tübingen in 2021 and graduated with distinction, ranking in the top 5% of my class. As a student, I worked part-time as a machine learning engineer at RAWLAB a , where engineering data processing start up, where I applied ML methods to time-series analysis problems. I am currently writing my Master's thesis about representation engineering in LLMs under the supervision of Seyed Ali Bahrainian and Carsten Eickhoff from the Health-NLP group at the University Tübingen and Dmitrii Krasheninnikov and David Krueger from the KASL group at the University of Cambridge.

Feel free to reach out to me via mail!
